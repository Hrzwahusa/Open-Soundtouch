Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026 · Applies to the “Open SoundTouch” Android app (com.hrzwahusa.opensoundtouch).

In short: Open SoundTouch does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the developer or to third parties. There are no accounts, no ads and no analytics. The app runs on your local Wi‑Fi network and talks to your own speakers.

1. Who we are

Open SoundTouch is a free, independent, open‑source app that lets you control Bose SoundTouch speakers on your local network after the official app was discontinued. It is provided by the app’s developer (“we”, “us”). Contact details are at the bottom of this page.

2. Data we collect

None. We do not operate any server, account system, ad network or analytics. We do not collect names, email addresses, contacts, location, advertising identifiers or usage statistics. We have no way to identify you.

3. Data stored on your device

The app saves a small amount of data locally on your device only, so it can remember your setup:

  • Discovered / added speakers (name, network address).
  • Your internet‑radio favorites.
  • Your multi‑room group definitions.

This information never leaves your device, is not sent to us, and is removed when you uninstall the app or clear its storage.

4. Network communication

The app communicates over the network only for the following purposes:

  • Your speakers (local network): commands, status, group setup and audio streaming go directly to the SoundTouch speakers on your own Wi‑Fi.
  • Internet‑radio search: when you search for stations, your search terms are sent to TuneIn’s public directory (opml.radiotime.com) to return results. This request is governed by TuneIn’s own privacy policy.
  • Radio playback: when you play a station, your speaker (or the app) connects to that station’s stream server to fetch the audio.

We do not route any of this traffic through our own servers — we don’t have any.

5. Permissions and why they are used

  • Internet / network & Wi‑Fi state: to find and control speakers on your local network and to search/stream internet radio.
  • Microphone / “record audio” (optional feature): used only if you start the “stream this phone’s audio to a speaker” feature. It captures the audio your phone is playing (via Android’s media‑projection screen‑capture consent) and sends it over your local network to your own speaker. This audio is not recorded to storage and is never sent to us or any third party. Nothing is captured unless you explicitly start it and confirm the system dialog.
  • Foreground service & notifications: to keep the audio stream running reliably and to show an ongoing notification (with volume controls) while it is active.

6. Sharing and selling of data

We do not sell, rent or share any data, because we do not collect any.

7. Children’s privacy

The app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

8. Changes to this policy

If the app’s behavior changes in a way that affects this policy, we will update this page and the “last updated” date above.
