Bose shut down the SoundTouch app and cloud. Open SoundTouch controls your speakers directly on your Wi‑Fi — internet radio, multi‑room groups, presets and more. No cloud, no account, nothing leaves your network.

Join the closed test View source on GitHub

Android app in closed testing. A speaker is set up once with the free desktop app (Windows) — then you control everything from your phone.

What it does

Everything the old app did — and a few things it never could.

🎛️

Full local control

Play/pause, skip, power and volume for every SoundTouch on your network — instantly, over Wi‑Fi.

📻

Internet radio

Search thousands of stations, save favorites, and stream them straight to your speaker.

🔗

Multi‑room groups

Create zones, play in sync, and adjust group volume or each speaker on its own.

⭐

Physical preset buttons

Assign your favorite stations to the 1–6 buttons on the speaker so they work reboot‑proof.

🎧

Stream your phone

Send any audio playing on your phone to a speaker or a whole group, and control volume with the hardware keys.

🔒

Private by design

No accounts, no ads, no analytics. The app talks only to your speakers and, for radio search, TuneIn.

How it works

A speaker is set up once with the desktop app — then it's three steps on your phone.

STEP 1

Connect

The app finds SoundTouch speakers on your Wi‑Fi automatically (or add one by IP).

STEP 2

Control & listen

Browse radio, build groups, set presets, or stream your phone — all on your network.

STEP 3

Keep it local

Everything runs on your LAN. Your favorites and groups stay on your device.
